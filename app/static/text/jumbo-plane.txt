<h2>You've earned the Jumbo Plane badge!</h2>
<br>
Did you know? The average long-haul flight takes up to 102g of CO2 per passenger for every kilometre travelled?
Surprisingly, short-haul flights can take up to twice the amount of CO2 per passenger since take-off and landing are where most of the emissions happen!
